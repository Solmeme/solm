Tokenomics

Community - 40%

Giveaways, Token airdrops and NFT mints to reward community engagement, liquidity provision, events participation and community engagement.

Expense & Dev - 30%

Fund Development projects, Marketing, Buybacks and Liquidity. All major spend proposals will require community voting on the blockchain.

Asset Reserve - 25%

Reserve wallets made public, managed by Team, and overseen by a Board -- with systems in place for approving transfers of tokens and assets.

Project Team - 5%

All compensations and airdrops to Team/OG members and Partners staggered in a sustainable fashion to keep the community invested.
